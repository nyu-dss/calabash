Calabash

A JOURNAL OF CARIBBEAN ARTS AND LETTERS Volume 5, Number 1: Summer/Fall 2008

Millicent A. Graham

YELLOW DOG

In the pitch black
shadow of a hill

the yellow dog rises, like a halo...
II

Under the tamarind tree
the grasses shoot-

the yellow dog digs them out furiously!
Il

The statue’s head is rolling-
the yellow dog is yelping,
I closed my eyes and whisper

in tandem, ‘Amen, amen.’
