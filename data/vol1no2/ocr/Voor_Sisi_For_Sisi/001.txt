For Sisi

The old forest path

that guided me to your river
is still there

My footprints

are dry and cracked

The root of the gbé- gbé
by the bank

— your favorite spot —

is empty

The beams of the late
afternoon sun

miss your naked back

and dance in the

rolling water

Sometimes, when homesickness
eats me up,

I think I see you

bending over the dishes
