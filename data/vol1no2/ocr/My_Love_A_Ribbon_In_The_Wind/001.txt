Nydia Ecury

URS

Mi amor,

un sinta den bientu.

M'a dobl'é, bolbe dobl’é,
sigui dobla,

te ku el a bira

grandura di un simia

ku mi por a skonde

den mi kurason.

Mi amor, manera un simia
bou di tera di mi kurason
warda.

Sperando e dia

p'el a bolbe brota
pusha

sali

subi

krese

bai ondia den bientu.

Un sinta hanchu,
sobenta yarda largu,
ta zoya den bientu.
Ta kanta

ta bati

ta balia

ta boltu

den bientu,
kambiando koldé.

Di kora
di sanger sarafina,

pa bérdeé

My Love,

a Ribbon in the Wind

I folded it
and folded it
until it was as small

as a seed
that I could tuck away

in my heart.

My love
like a seed
covered

by the earth
of my heart.

There awaited the day
to burst forth

once more

to push

to rise

to climb

to grow

and go waving

in the wind.

A wide wide ribbon
endlessly long

swaying in the wind.
Singing

beating

dancing

frolicking in the wind
while changing colours.

From red
of blood undeniable
to green
