which fueled his young spirit

and ate up his appetite for school

till he learned to bluff his way

through a world of tales, and swapped

Sunlight Street for the narrowing lane,

alive with ground lizards

variegated as croton

insinuating themselves through the loose dirt

to the gully bank where the daggers

of the cactuses’ bristles shadowed the path.
