Many fourth and fifth generation Indo-Caribbeans are tracing their ancestral
roots and are visiting their fore-parents homes/villages in India. Those who are running
away from their identity seem to have forgotten that others define them as Indians, and
though matter what they call themselves in the cosmopolitan Caribbean, Indians cannot
