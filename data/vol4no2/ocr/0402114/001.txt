Mervyn Morris

THE LOVERS

(after René Magritte)
eee

The lovers kissing
do not see
each other, do not feel

unmediated hair and skin.

Each hooded
in an opaque swirl,
neither seems aware

of something strange.

Do not disturb.
They are in love.
Each feels a kinship

with the other’s mask.
