SIDNEY’S KIDNEYS

His heart was

successfully bypassed

but his kidneys

did him in.

Perhaps there was

too much water:
a mean wife
dead daughter
schized daughter
runaway son

Sidney’s kidneys
shut down, too many
backed up

tears.

Garrison / 88
